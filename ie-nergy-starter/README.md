# IE-nergy Spring Campaign Landing Page

A one-page signup site for the spring launch of **IE-nergy** — a fictional energy drink aimed at IE Business School students. Visitors land on a hero shot of the can, pick their campus and preferred flavour, and drop their email to be among the first notified when the drink launches. Signups flow straight into a Mailchimp audience with double opt-in and explicit GDPR consent, so the follow-up campaign only ever emails people who confirmed they want to hear from us. Built as part of the IE Business School AI Marketing Lab — scaffolded, styled, wired, and deployed entirely through Claude Code.
